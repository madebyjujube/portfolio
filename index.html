jujube (music)

This website was created for an event series called WAVES which took place during the summer of 2021, and which synced with new and full moon cycles. WAVES was mainly a space created for people to harness the therapeutic benefits of sound in a setting specifically for curated for that.

This is a website that was created for WAVES, an event series that took place during the summer of 2021. WAVES offered a curated space where people could experience the therapeutic benefits of sound. The events were synchronized with new and full moon cycles, enhancing the transformative power of the sound-based encounters.

During the new moons, WAVES offered ambient shows accompanied by guided meditations. These experiences provided a sanctuary for relaxation and inner reflection.

On the nights of the full moons, WAVES transformed into a celebration of techno music and dancing. With pulsating rhythms and raw energy, attendees indulged in an exhilarating fusion of music and movement.

~about jujube

Juju (aka jujube) is both an electronic musician and visual artist, traversing a creative path that seamlessly merges infectious rhythms with captivating textures. The sonic and tactile dimensions of their work create an immersive experience that encourages dance and contemplation in equal measure.

Currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree at Concordia University in Tio'tia'ke, Montreal, with majors in Electroacoustics and Design, juju has finely cultivated their ability in curating experiences that blur the boundaries between sound and aesthetics. Adept across a spectrum of disciplines encompassing front-end web development, typography, sound engineering, recording, and production, they infuse their creative endeavors with a comprehensive and unwavering artistic passion.

Their work invites individuals to surrender to the groove, engage with the textures, and embark on a shared expedition of creativity.
